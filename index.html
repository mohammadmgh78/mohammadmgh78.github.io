About

I am a Ph.D. student in Data Science at Texas A&M University working at the intersection of machine learning, optimization, and simulation.

My research focuses on AI-driven decision making, reinforcement learning, digital twins, and probabilistic modeling for complex stochastic systems.

I collaborate with the Intuit AI Research team on large-scale operational decision systems, combining deep learning, simulation optimization, and uncertainty quantification. My work integrates reinforcement learning, surrogate modeling, and stochastic forecasting to improve real-world large-scale operational performance.

My long-term goal is to develop intelligent decision-making systems that combine operations research with modern AI methods to optimize complex stochastic systems and digital twins.
